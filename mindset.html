Our mindset is the foundation of everything we do. It shapes how we think, feel, and respond to challenges. A positive mindset helps us see possibilities, learn from failure, and stay motivated even when things get tough.

According to psychologist Carol Dweck, there are two main types of mindsets: fixed and growth. A fixed mindset believes abilities are static and unchangeable, while a growth mindset believes we can improve with effort and learning.

People with a growth mindset are more likely to embrace challenges, persist through obstacles, and develop resilience. They see failure not as a sign to quit, but as an opportunity to grow.
